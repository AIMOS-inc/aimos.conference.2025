Logistics

The main conference will be at the Australian National University (ANU) Acton campus in Canberra. Canberra is in the Australian Capital Territory.

Interactive Google Map:

Map of the ANU Acton campus with Hedley Bull Building (Building 130) highlighted:

The conference will take place at the Asia Pacific College of Diplomacy lecture theatre, Ground Floor, Hedley Bull Building (130 Garran Rd, Acton ACT 2601).

Wi-Fi

Complimentary Wi-Fi will be available throughout the conference. Login details will be available at the conference.

Accessibility

We aim to make AIMOS2024 an accessible conference for all, and this includes our social events. As an attendee, you play an important role in this. For example, we can all commit to using a microphone when we ask questions, so that everyone can hear. We will endeavour to make microphones easily available.

Free disability parking is available for those with a valid Australian mobility permit or an ANU mobility permit. Disability parking spaces on campus are clearly signposted. The ANU Designated parking areas map shows locations of disability parking spaces.

For more information on making your way around campus, please see here.

Getting to Canberra

Flights: Canberra has its own airport (code CBR). You can see all the direct flights to Canberra here. International travellers will likely need to fly via Sydney or Melbourne.

Driving: You can use this site to find the driving distances and times between Canberra and any place in Australia.

Public transport

The number 53 ACTON bus stops on Liversidge Street after Ellery Crescent. Plan your journey to and from campus with journey planner.

Bicycle parking

There are two bicycle racks located outside the building. Ensure to secure your bike by locking the frame and both wheels of your bicycle to a hoop using heavy duty lock or chain.

Car parking

Parking is available on Liversidge Road in Pay As You Go parking zones (see parking fees). There are a limited number of parking spaces, so please arrive early to secure a parking spot.

Visa to enter Australia

If you're coming from overseas then you will need a visa to enter Australia. This will most likely be an eVisitor visa, which is free and allows you to attend a conference. You can check what visa is best for you here. Please get in touch if you need a letter of support.

Places to stay

To stay close to the conference venue, look for places in the suburbs of Acton, Braddon or Canberra.

Weather

According to the Bureau of Meteorology the average November temperatures in Canberra range from 10 to 25 degrees C. The seven-day bureau forecast is here.
