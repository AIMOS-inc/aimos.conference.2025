Proposals

Submissions for the conference are open from Thursday 1st August 2025. Please use the Google form here to submit. Submissions will close on Monday 2nd September at midnight Australian Eastern Standard Time.

We will accept submissions in the following categories:

  • Lightning talks - Short talks of no longer than five minutes to be presented in "Lightning Panel" groups.
  • Workshops - Should be presented by experienced researchers who give guided instruction and training on a particular topic.
  • Hackathons - A hands-on project with a clear end goal.
  • Discussion groups - Unstructured but with a focal issue or question.
