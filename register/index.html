Early bird rates (on or before 31 October):

Having an early bird greatly helps us plan for catering and rooms, so please register early.

  • Full in-person - AUD $200.
  • Concession in-person - AUD $100. This is for current students (full-time or part-time who spend at least 50% of their time on study), retirees, or unwaged.

Full rates (from 1 November):

  • Full in-person - AUD $250.
  • Concession in-person - AUD $150. This is for current students (full-time or part-time who spend at least 50% of their time on study), retirees, or unwaged.

Virtual attendance is free.

In-person attendance includes lunch and morning/afternoon tea.

Payment and registration

You can register and pay using Humantix here.

If you are in financial difficulties but would still like to attend in-person, then please email the AIMOS president (aimos.inc@gmail.com) with a brief outline of your circumstances. We can provide a letter of support to show to your employer/institution.

Paying for conference attendance means that you'll automatically be an AIMOS member for one year.

You'll be sent a confirmation email once the payment has been processed.

Refunds

Refunds are available up to 1 day prior to the event.

Virtual attendance
